        DOSSIER
        
Cindy Gravity is an experimental pop trio moving
          between psychedelic pop, R’n’B, and yacht rock. Following their debut
          Brutally Soft, the band is entering a darker chapter with their new
          album HOT GODS.

        
The songs explore inner demons, spiritual
          experiences, and ritual magic. With an eclectic arsenal of drum
          machines, breakbeats, Japanese synthesizers, and surf guitars, Cindy
          Gravity channels three-part harmonies into pop songs about the
          synchronicity of the universe.

        
More fearless than ever, the trio keeps pushing their
          sound. Beautiful pop melodies meet occult imagery, bold hooks dissolve
          into dream logic and surreal storytelling.
