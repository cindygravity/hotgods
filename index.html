This Is Cindy Gravity

Cindy Gravity is an experimental pop trio that established itself as a boundary-crosser between psychedelic pop, R’n’B, and yacht rock with their debut “Brutally Soft.”

In 2025, Cindy Gravity presents their new album HOT GODS, in which they explore a darker side. The themes revolve around inner demons, spiritual experiences, and ritual magic.

Cindy Gravity has further developed, condensed, and refined their sound. With an eclectic arsenal of drum machines, breakbeats, Japanese synthesizers and surf guitars, the trio sings three-voiced harmonies on pop songs about the synchronicity of the universe.
